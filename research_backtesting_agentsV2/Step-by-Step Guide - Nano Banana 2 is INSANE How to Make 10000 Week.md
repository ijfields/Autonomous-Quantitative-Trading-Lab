## Business Model 2: AI Influencer Agency

### Step 1: Create an AI Persona
1. Generate a consistent face using reference images
2. Give the persona a name, backstory, and personality

### Step 2: Build the Social Presence
1. Create new Instagram/social account
2. **First week:** Only like/comment on other posts (warm up, avoid bot detection)
3. **After 1 week:** Start posting 2-3 times daily, every single day
4. Never miss a day — consistency is non-negotiable

### Step 3: Monetize
1. Brand deals once following grows
2. Affiliate links in bio/posts
3. Merchandise, Ko-fi, Patreon
4. Scale to managing 10+ AI influencer accounts
