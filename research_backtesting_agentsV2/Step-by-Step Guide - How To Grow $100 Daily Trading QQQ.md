# Step-by-Step Guide: How To Grow $100 Daily Trading QQQ

**Source:** Investment Gains (YouTube)
**Video ID:** mZJl6mYDf1w
**Upload Date:** 2024-06-22

## Prerequisites
- **Broker:** Options-approved brokerage with QQQ options access
- **Knowledge:** Basic understanding of put credit spreads, probability of profit
- **Capital:** Enough to cover max loss on spread (6 contracts x $100 spread width = $600 max risk before credit)

---

## Step 1: Open Options Chain for QQQ
Navigate to QQQ → Trade → Options on your brokerage platform.

## Step 2: Select Strike Date
Choose an expiration date. In the example, the speaker selected June 21st.

## Step 3: Select the Sell Put Strike
- Choose "Sell" and "Put"
- Select a strike price **below the current share price** with **80% or higher probability of profit**
- Example: Sold the 481 put

## Step 4: Select the Buy Put Strike
- Choose "Buy" and "Put"
- Select a strike price **$1 below your sell put** for protection
- Example: Bought the 480 put
- This creates a $1-wide put credit spread

## Step 5: Scale Contracts to Hit Target
- Increase number of contracts until credit reaches ~$100
- Example: 6 contracts = $101 total credit
- Credit per contract varies by market conditions

## Step 6: Review and Submit
Hit "Review" and confirm the order. Rinse and repeat daily.

---

## Common Pitfalls
- **No stop-loss mentioned:** The video does not discuss what to do if the trade goes against you
- **No position sizing guidance:** Max loss relative to account size is not addressed
- **DTE selection not explained:** Why this particular expiration was chosen is unclear
- **Market condition filters absent:** No guidance on when NOT to place the trade
