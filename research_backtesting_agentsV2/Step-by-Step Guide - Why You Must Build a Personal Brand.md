## What This Guide Covers

A 3-phase framework for building a personal brand: discover stories, test with content, scale with opt-in funnels.

---

## Phase 1: Discover Your Unique Stories

1. Open your phone's photo gallery in month-by-month view
2. Scroll through the last **60 months**
3. Identify at least **10 stories** with transferable lessons
4. For each story, ask: "What lesson would my target audience value?"
5. Use AI to refine: "Ask me questions and help me distill the lessons"
6. Remember: **relatable beats impressive** — share next-step stories, not unicorn achievements

---

## Phase 2: Test with Content

### Short-Form (3x/week minimum)
- Under 3 minutes
- Use one of three hook frameworks:
  - **Pain:** A problem → explain why it happens and what to do
  - **Prize:** Something desirable → show how to achieve it
  - **News:** Public event → provide unique analysis
- CTA: "Drop me a DM"
- Goal: 30 real conversations to learn what resonates

### Long-Form (Regular cadence)
- YouTube, podcasts, newsletters, blogs
- Focus on **teaching a process** that solves the pain or achieves the prize
- This is your signature content — what you become known for

---

## Phase 3: Scale to 7-8 Figures

### Build Opt-In Mechanisms
1. **Waiting list** — create scarcity (Rolex model)
2. **Assessment** — interactive quiz that provides personalized results
3. **Webinar** — 45-90 min deep-dive step-by-step
4. **Mini-course** — structured multi-lesson content

### The Funnel
```
Short-form content (personal brand, 20x reach)
  → Long-form content (process teaching)
    → CTA / Opt-in (business brand enters here)
      → Pipeline: warm leads → sales calls → conversion
```

---

## Key Takeaway

> There is a 1-2 year window before AI content saturation closes the door for new personal brands. Your lived experience is the one thing AI cannot replicate. Start with phone photo story discovery, test with 3x/week short-form content, and scale with opt-in funnels. Personal brands get 20x more social reach than business brands.
