## Step 1: Understand the Three Drivers of AI Advancement
1. **Compute scaling** -- massive investments in physical processing capacity by multiple nations; measured in gigawatts and dollars
2. **Algorithmic improvements** -- changes to models, mathematics, and approaches that make AI more efficient
3. **Unhobbling** -- the critical factor: adding memory, chain-of-thought reasoning, persistence, agency, and multi-model coordination to currently "hobbled" AI systems
4. These three factors compound, driving half an order-of-magnitude improvement approximately every six months

## Step 2: Grasp the AGI-to-Superintelligence Trajectory
1. AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) = AI that can do anything a remote human worker can do, but with superhuman speed
2. Leopold predicts AGI is plausible by 2027 based on extrapolation from GPT-2 to GPT-4 scaling trends
3. AGI is a stepping stone, not the endpoint -- the "intelligence explosion" follows
4. Superintelligence = AI vastly smarter than humans, potentially "a small civilization of billions of AGIs"
5. Dario Amodei (Anthropic CEO) has expressed similar timeline predictions

## Step 3: Recognize the National Security Dimension
1. AI model weights are the critical asset -- if acquired, a competitor can replicate capabilities without doing the research
2. Current AI knowledge sharing in Silicon Valley (including at informal gatherings) is largely unsecured
3. Leopold argues this is analogous to pre-Manhattan Project nuclear physics -- currently open but soon requiring security
4. The paper warns against waiting for a crisis to implement security measures
5. Unlike the Manhattan Project, there is no single galvanizing event (like a war) driving government action yet

## Step 4: Avoid the "Incremental Improvement" Trap in Business
1. Do NOT assume the next generation of AI is just a slightly better version of today's tools
2. The improvements are measured in orders of magnitude, not percentages
3. Leopold's token scaling analysis: current AI handles "a few minutes of work" worth of tokens; within years, AI could handle "multiple months of work" in a single context
4. Plan for transformative change, not incremental improvement

## Step 5: Prepare Your Business Data Now
1. Save all transcripts, conversations, and interactions -- even if you do not know how to use them yet
2. Collect multi-modal data: audio, video, text, and images
3. Implement data governance: label, organize, and structure your data properly
4. Clean your data house -- ensure data quality and accessibility
5. Rationale: soon AI models will be capable of analyzing months or years of business data in depth; companies that have prepared their data will have a massive advantage over those that start gathering data after the fact

## Step 6: Stay Informed on AI Development
1. Read Leopold Aschenbrenner's "Situational Awareness" paper in full (available at situational-awareness.ai or similar)
2. Follow Leopold on X/Twitter for ongoing analysis
3. Watch Dario Amodei's interview with Ezra Klein for complementary predictions from an industry insider
4. Follow "The Daily AI Show" for regular panel discussions on AI developments
5. Consider the contrast between Leopold's cautious activism and Yann LeCun's skepticism -- form your own informed view
