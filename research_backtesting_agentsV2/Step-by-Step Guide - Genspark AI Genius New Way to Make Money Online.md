## What This Guide Covers

How to build a multi-component lead magnet funnel using Genspark AI, embed an affiliate upsell, deploy with one click, and drive traffic via Pinterest.

---

## Step 1: Join an Affiliate Program

- Example: **Preply** (online language tutoring) — earn $20+ per trial lesson booked
- Choose programs targeting high-intent audiences actively searching for solutions

---

## Step 2: Create the Lead Magnet (3 Components)

### Component 1: Phrase Guide Slide Deck
1. Open Genspark AI Slides with "Nano Banana Pro" in creative mode
2. Prompt: "10 must know Japanese phrases"
3. Export as PDF to Genspark AI Drive

### Component 2: Audio Companion Web App
1. Switch to AI Developer ("simple website or web app" mode)
2. Upload the exported slide deck from AI Drive
3. Prompt: create audio companion with play buttons, voice selection, speed controls
4. Result: functional web app with native pronunciation playback

### Component 3: Interactive Quiz
1. Still in AI Developer, prompt: "Create an interactive quiz testing knowledge of those Japanese phrases"
2. Add follow-up prompt: insert Preply affiliate upsell at end of quiz
3. Users see their score → call-to-action → affiliate link

---

## Step 3: Combine and Deploy

1. Prompt AI Developer to merge all three into one "Language Learning Kit" landing page
2. Click **Hosted Deploy** to publish to a public URL
3. No external hosting, domain, or technical setup needed

---

## Step 4: Create Pinterest Traffic

1. Use **AI Image** (Nano Banana 2.0) to generate a Pinterest pin
2. Include: headline, relevant image, CTA mentioning free audio + quiz
3. Use Pinterest autocomplete for keyword research
4. Feed keywords into Genspark to generate optimized pin title and description
5. Publish pin with lead magnet URL

---

## Step 5: Scale

### Multi-Language Translation
- Use Genspark to translate entire kit into French, German, etc.
- Each translation targets a new audience segment

### Email Capture
- Add email form to landing page (prompt AI Developer)
- Collect name + email before kit access
- Send automated follow-up emails with additional affiliate offers

---

## Key Takeaway

> The core insight is that most affiliate marketers offer low-effort freebies. Genspark enables creating interactive, multi-component resources (slides + audio + quiz) that dramatically outperform static PDFs, driving higher engagement and conversion — all from a single tool with one-click deployment.
