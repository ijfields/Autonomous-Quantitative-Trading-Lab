## Strategy Overview

A short-biased Ethereum futures strategy that fades rallies into resistance zones when multiple confirmation signals align: declining ETH/BTC ratio, institutional outflows from Ethereum investment products, and decreasing open interest on ETH perpetuals. The strategy was AI-generated from one day's news cycle and demonstrated in the video as both a trading implementation and interview-prep scenario. Brian generated it using Qwen Coder and implemented it as a full Python trading bot against the Rithmic API.

## Entry Rules

1. **Resistance zone identification:** Price enters a 1.5-2.5% resistance zone measured from the local low.
2. **ETH/BTC ratio confirmation:** The ETH/BTC ratio must be making new lows (indicates Ethereum underperforming Bitcoin).
3. **Institutional flow confirmation:** Significant outflows from Ethereum investment products (example trigger: $36M outflow).
4. **Open interest confirmation:** Decreasing open interest on Ethereum perpetual contracts (indicates less conviction from longs, setting up for accelerated breakdown).
5. **Volume confirmation:** Volume must confirm the rejection at resistance.
6. **Funding rate monitoring:** Funding rates monitored for additional short bias confirmation.

## Exit Rules

- **Target:** Not explicitly stated; implied profit-taking at support levels.
- **Stop-loss:** Not explicitly quantified; the bot includes stop targets in its generated logic.
- **Avoid/filter condition:** Do NOT enter if BTC simultaneously reclaims its own 2% resistance level (this would invalidate the bearish thesis for ETH).
