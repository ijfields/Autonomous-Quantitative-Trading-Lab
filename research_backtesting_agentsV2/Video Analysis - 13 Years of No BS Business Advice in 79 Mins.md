### CLOSER Sales Framework (Detail)
- **C** — Clarify why they're there
- **L** — Label the problem ("So it sounds like...")
- **O** — Overview past experiences (increase deprivation)
- **S** — Sell the vacation (not the plane flight — outcome, not features, 3-point pitch)
- **E** — Explain away concerns (specifics, time, money, decision-maker, avoidance)
- **R** — Reinforce the decision (overdeliver in first 24 hours)

### Pricing Strategy
- Warren Buffett raised See's Candies prices for 50 years (up to 177% in one year), clearing $1B in profit
- If prices unchanged since 2017, inflation has erased ~20% of margin
- Test 2x-4x price increases; grandfather existing customers with price increase letter

## ACTIONABLE TAKEAWAYS

1. Raise prices by at least inflation annually (3-6%); test 2-4x increases
2. Use the CLOSER framework for sales calls
3. Build an indexed content library for sales objection handling
4. Unify sales and marketing under one revenue leader
5. Bill customers for longer periods upfront (annual with guarantee)
6. Talk to 10+ customers/non-buyers per week
7. Do 10x more of what works before trying new channels
8. Promote the person with highest standards, not longest tenure
