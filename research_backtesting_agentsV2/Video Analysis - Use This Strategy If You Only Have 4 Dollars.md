### The 5-Step Entry Checklist

#### Step 1: Key Support/Resistance Zones (15-Min Chart)

**When:** ~9:15 AM ET (15 minutes before market open)

- Mark major swing highs and lows on the 15-minute chart
- Draw boxes around zones where price has reacted before
- Include trend lines (upward/downward resistance/support)
- These are areas where buyers/sellers have historically stepped in
- **Purpose:** Establishes the "big picture bias" — where is price likely to reverse?

#### Step 2: Identify an Unhealthy/Overextended Move

- Wait for price to spike into one of the key zones
- An "unhealthy move" = fast, extended spike (big candlesticks, rapid price change)
- **Key insight:** "When the market makes a big move up, if it reverses, it reverses in a big way"
- Don't try to predict the move — wait for it to happen, then trade the reversal
- This move provides the catalyst for a reversal trade

#### Step 3: Trend Break on 1-Min Chart

- Drop to the 1-minute chart and watch the trend structure
- In an uptrend: higher highs + higher lows
- **First reversal signal:** A lower low (first break of the uptrend structure)
- **Confirmation:** A lower high follows the lower low
- **Critical rule:** Swings must be multi-candlestick moves, NOT single-candle blips
  - Wrong: one-candlestick lower high → doesn't count
  - Right: multiple candlesticks forming a swing → counts as a legitimate lower high

#### Step 4: Confirmation (New Low/High Break)

- Wait for a momentum confirmation: big bearish candlestick breaking the previous low (for shorts)
- Sometimes enter on the first break; sometimes wait for pullback + re-break
- **Bait detection:** Market sometimes makes a new low then immediately reverses (bait/trap)
- Extra confirmation: wait for pullback after the new low, then enter on the next lower move
- This is the "hardest part of trading" — picking the top/bottom — but the checklist reduces guesswork

#### Step 5: Entry with Risk-Reward Assessment

- Use a sell stop order (shorts) or buy stop order (longs) at the break level
- **Before entering:** Verify that the potential reward is at least 2-3x the risk
- Stop-loss: Above/below the swing that confirmed the reversal
- If R:R doesn't meet minimum 1:2 (ideally 1:3), skip the trade

### Trade Management (Fluid Trailing Stop)

- **Initial phase:** Set stop at the confirming swing level; let trade develop
- **Support/resistance approach:** When price nears initial target (start of unhealthy move), assess whether it's a minor or major level
- **Momentum phase:** When price accelerates, trail stop to each previous candlestick's high/low as each candle closes
- **Exit zone:** When price reaches a major support/resistance zone on the 15-min chart, expect a bounce and tighten management
- **Key warning:** "Unhealthy moves in your favor will also reverse" — don't get greedy on momentum
