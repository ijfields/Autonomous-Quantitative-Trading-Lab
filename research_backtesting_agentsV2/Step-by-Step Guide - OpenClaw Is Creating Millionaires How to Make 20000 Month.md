## What This Guide Covers

How to build a local business selling AI agent setup and management services to small businesses using OpenClaw.

---

## Step 1: Learn the Tools (1 Hour/Day)
1. Spend at least 1 hour daily using ChatGPT, Claude, Grok, Gemini
2. Give them real tasks, push their limits, become fluent
3. Learn OpenClaw basics: terminal/bash/SSH, agent configuration, Telegram/WhatsApp connections

## Step 2: Pick a Niche
1. Choose ONE specific small business vertical:
   - Dentists, restaurants, real estate agents, salons, gyms, law offices
2. Learn their workflows, pain points, and what they waste time/money on
3. Do NOT try to serve everyone — specificity wins

## Step 3: Build Your Service Offering
1. **AI chatbot setup** — customer service, lead capture, appointment booking
2. **Automated social media** — content creation, scheduling, engagement
3. **Email/CRM automation** — follow-up sequences, lead nurturing
4. **Custom agent workflows** — whatever the niche needs most

## Step 4: Price Your Services
1. **One-time setup fee:** $5,000 (install agents on client's local hardware or VPS)
2. **Monthly management:** $1,500/month (monitoring, updates, new workflows)
3. **Target:** 4-5 clients for comfortable income, 10 clients for $15K/month recurring

## Step 5: Find Clients
1. Local outreach — visit businesses in person, demonstrate AI capabilities live
2. Show them tasks their employees spend hours on that AI can do in seconds
3. Position yourself as "the AI person" in your market
4. Be the bridge between the technology and the people who need it

## Step 6: Scale to One-Person Agency
1. Use AI agents for your own business operations (meta-automation)
2. Agent-to-agent communication for client management
3. Expand to managing 10-20+ clients using AI to handle most of the work

---

## Key Takeaway

> The early adopter window is ~6 months before consumer-friendly autonomous agents arrive. Learn now, pick a niche, start selling immediately — even if imperfect. Being early matters more than being perfect.
