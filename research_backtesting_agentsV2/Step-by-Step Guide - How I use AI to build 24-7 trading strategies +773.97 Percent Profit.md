## What This Guide Covers

## Step 1: Access the Agency Agents Repository
1. Go to GitHub and search for "Agency Agents" by Mr. Razeki
2. Star the repository for easy reference
3. Browse the list of 51 specialized agent personas
4. Identify which roles are most relevant to your business needs

## Step 2: Understand the Persona Categories
1. Review the available business personas:
   - Marketing Director -- brand positioning, market segmentation, competitive analysis
   - Sales Manager -- lead qualification, objection handling, pipeline management, conversion metrics
   - Technical Debt Specialist -- code auditing, architecture review, identifying future failure points
   - Customer Success Manager -- retention and expansion strategies
   - Product Manager -- roadmaps, user stories, feature prioritization
   - DevOps Engineer -- infrastructure decisions, deployment strategies
   - Crisis Communication Specialist -- messaging during incidents, narrative control
   - AI Ethics Adviser -- bias, privacy, transparency considerations
   - Content Strategy Specialist -- content pillars, editorial calendars, distribution strategies
   - UX Designer -- user flows, interface decisions
   - Financial Analyst -- scenario modeling, unit economics
   - Legal Affairs Specialist -- contracts, compliance
   - Sustainability Consultant -- environmental considerations in operations
2. Select the personas that fill gaps in your current team or knowledge

## Step 3: Load a Persona into Your AI Tool
1. Open your preferred AI tool (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, or similar)
2. Copy the full persona prompt from the GitHub repository
3. Paste it into the system prompt or initial message
4. Begin interacting with the AI as if it were a real team member in that role
5. Example: Instead of saying "Help me with marketing," say "I'm launching a new SaaS product. What should my go-to-market strategy be?"
6. The agent will ask contextual questions (target market, budget, competitive landscape) just like a real professional would

## Step 4: Customize Personas for Your Business
1. Fork or clone the repository to make your own modifications
2. Add industry-specific knowledge, company context, and proprietary frameworks
3. Adjust the persona's priorities to match your business stage (startup vs. scaling vs. enterprise)
4. Save your customized personas for reuse across projects

## Step 5: Build a Multi-Agent Advisory Board
1. Select 3-5 personas most critical to your business
2. Set each up as a separate conversation or agent instance
3. When facing a decision, consult multiple agents for different perspectives:
   - Marketing Director for go-to-market strategy
   - Financial Analyst for unit economics
   - Technical Debt Specialist for architecture review
   - Customer Success Manager for retention planning
4. Compare and synthesize their recommendations

## Step 6: Integrate with OpenClaw (Optional Advanced Setup)
1. If using OpenClaw, create persistent agents using these personas
2. Assign each persona to a department with its own communication channel
3. Set up daily cron jobs for automated analysis and reporting
4. Have a coordinator agent (chief of staff) synthesize outputs from all department agents
