## What This Guide Covers

How to use OpenClaw to automate wholesaling real estate tasks: data scraping, skip tracing, buyer outreach, campaign analysis, and daily reporting.

---

## Step 1: Set Up Dedicated Hardware

1. Buy a **Mac Mini** (~$600) or dedicated MacBook
2. **Never use your personal computer** — OpenClaw has full computer control
3. Only give the dedicated machine access to what the bot needs
4. Connect to ChatGPT Plus (~$15/month) or other AI provider via API

---

## Step 2: Train the Bot on Your Business

1. Export your ChatGPT conversation history and feed it to OpenClaw
2. Have the bot watch your training/mentorship videos
3. Provide your wholesaling methodology, scripts, and templates
4. The bot learns your specific approach before executing tasks

---

## Step 3: Automate Core Wholesaling Tasks

### Data Pulling
- Pull property data, county records, tax information
- Build targeted lists for outreach campaigns

### Skip Tracing LLCs
- Find the actual people behind LLC-owned properties
- Identify cash buyers for direct outreach

### Buyer Outreach
- Automated texting and emailing to cash buyers
- Integrate via WhatsApp/Telegram for VA-like workflow

### Daily Reporting
- Analyze sales team performance and marketing metrics
- Text you a daily summary report

---

## Step 4: Choose Your Interaction Method

| Method | Best For |
|--------|----------|
| Terminal commands | Direct, specific instructions |
| WhatsApp/Telegram | Team communication integration |
| Voice notes from phone | On-the-go task delegation |

---

## Key Takeaway

> OpenClaw replaces multiple VAs for repetitive wholesaling tasks (data scraping, skip tracing, outreach, reporting) at ~$15/month vs. hundreds in VA costs. The non-negotiable requirement: run it on a dedicated machine with limited access, never on your personal computer.
