## Key Concepts

### Triple EMA System
- **5 EMA (hot pink):** Fastest — first to react to price changes
- **11 EMA (red):** Medium speed — confirms direction
- **18 EMA (blue):** Slowest — defines the primary trend direction
- All three pointing northeast = strong bullish; all pointing southwest = strong bearish

### RSI(11) Settings
- **11-period RSI** (not standard 14) — catches faster turns
- Above 80 = overbought (bullish, not necessarily a sell)
- Below 25 = oversold (expect a bounce, not necessarily a buy)
- RSI works best at extremes; middle readings are less informative

### Air Gap Concept
- When price extends significantly above the 18 EMA, a large "air gap" forms
- This signals elevated risk of a pullback — do NOT enter new long positions here
- Wait for price to consolidate back toward the 18 EMA before entering

### Signal vs. Trigger Distinction
- **Signal:** Divergence or pattern that warns of a potential move (e.g., bearish RSI divergence)
- **Trigger:** Price action that confirms the move (e.g., uptrend line break)
- Never trade on a signal alone — always wait for the trigger

## Backtestable Parameters
- **Instruments:** QQQ, QLD (2x leveraged), TQQQ (3x leveraged)
- **Timeframe:** Daily chart
- **EMAs:** 5, 11, 18 (exponential)
- **RSI:** 11-period; overbought >80, oversold <25

### Sell Rules
- **Sell signal:** Bearish RSI divergence (price higher highs, RSI lower highs)
- **Sell trigger:** Price breaks short-term uptrend line AFTER divergence
- **Additional confirmation:** 5 and 11 EMA curling downwards
- **Air gap warning:** If price is extended far above 18 EMA, risk is elevated

### Buy Rules
- **Buy signal:** 5 EMA crosses above 11 EMA, then both cross above 18 EMA
- **Buy confirmation:** RSI breaks above recent peaks (above 60-65 zone)
- **All three EMAs pointing northeast** = strongest buy
- **Alternative entry:** Wait for 1-2-3 pullback pattern after crossover (initial move up, pullback to test area, then continuation)
- **Hammer candle** at support during pullback is bullish confirmation

### RSI Trend Confirmation
- RSI moving upward in same direction as price = genuine bullish move
- RSI peak breaking above prior RSI peaks = bullish momentum alive
- RSI overbought = bullish (NOT a sell signal by itself)

### Risk Management
- **Stop-loss:** Below recent swing low (example: entry at 409, stop at 390-392)
- **Trail stops:** Raise stops as price advances (move to just below most recent candle)
- **Take profit:** When air gap becomes excessive or bearish divergence + trigger appears
