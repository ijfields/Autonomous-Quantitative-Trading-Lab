## KEY TOPICS

### Part 1: Discover Your Unique Stories

- AI has consumed all published knowledge but has never *lived* a single day
- Your competitive advantage = personal stories with transferable lessons
- **"Pause, Reflect, Document" process:** Open phone photos (month-by-month), scroll 60 months, identify 10+ stories
- Steven Bartlett insight: "Relatable beats impressive" — relatable stories get 5-7x more views
- Use AI to refine stories with prompt: "Ask me questions and help me distill the lessons"

### Part 2: Test with Content

| Type | Format | Frequency | Hook Frameworks |
|------|--------|-----------|----------------|
| **Short-form** | <3 min video | 3x/week (Mon/Wed/Fri) | Pain, Prize, or News |
| **Long-form** | YouTube, podcasts, newsletters | Regular | Teach a process |

- **Pain:** A problem people experience → you explain why and what to do
- **Prize:** Something people want → you show how to get it
- **News:** Public event/figure → you provide unique analysis
- CTA: "Drop me a DM" → aim for 30 real conversations to learn what resonates

### Part 3: Scale to 7-8 Figures

Funnel: Short-form (daily) → Long-form (signature content) → CTA (opt-in) → Pipeline

Opt-in mechanisms:
- **Never-ending waiting list** (Rolex model)
- **Assessments** (e.g., Key Person of Influence test)
- **Webinars** (45-90 min step-by-step)
- **Mini-courses**

Personal brand drives short/long-form (20x more social reach); business brand enters at opt-in level only.

### Forward-Looking

- **Algorithmic media** (not social): platforms surface by topic interest, not friend connections
- **AIO replacing SEO**: content recommended by ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini
- **AI ads imminent** (Sam Altman confirmed): 800M searches/week on ChatGPT; early adopters get cheap leads

## ACTIONABLE TAKEAWAYS

1. **Start now** — 1-2 year window before AI content saturation
2. **Phone photo review** for story discovery — 60 months, 10+ stories
3. **Relatable beats impressive** — share next-step stories, not unicorn achievements
4. **3x/week short-form** with Pain/Prize/News hooks
5. **Teach processes** in long-form content
6. **"Drop me a DM" CTA** — target 30 real conversations
7. **Personal brand for reach, business brand for conversion** — never mix
8. **AIO is the new SEO** — optimize for AI recommendation engines
