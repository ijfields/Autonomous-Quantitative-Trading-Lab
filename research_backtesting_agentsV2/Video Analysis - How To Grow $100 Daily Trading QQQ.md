---

## Key Concepts

### Put Credit Spread Mechanics
- Sell a put below current price (high probability of expiring worthless)
- Buy a put $1 below the sold put for protection
- Collect credit as income; max loss is spread width minus credit

### Probability-Based Strike Selection
- Choose strike with 80%+ probability of profit
- Further OTM = higher probability but less credit per contract
- Scale number of contracts to hit daily income target
