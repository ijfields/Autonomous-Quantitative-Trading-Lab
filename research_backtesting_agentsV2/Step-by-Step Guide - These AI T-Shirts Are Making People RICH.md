# Step-by-Step Guide: AI Print-on-Demand T-Shirt Business with Nano Banana 2

**Source:** ENERGI (YouTube)

---

## What This Guide Covers

How to build a print-on-demand t-shirt business using AI for niche research, design generation (Nano Banana 2), and an independent e-commerce store (Hostinger + Printful).

---

## Step 1: Discover Underserved Niches with AI

1. Open Claude or ChatGPT (free tiers work)
2. Prompt: "Give me underserved micro-niches for t-shirt designs" — include your interests and experiences for tailored results
3. Look for identity-driven niches (night shift nurses, ADHD adults, female electricians, etc.)

---

## Step 2: Research Bestsellers on Etsy

1. Search Etsy for your niche keyword (e.g., "nomad t-shirt")
2. In the URL filter bar, change "stareller" to "bestseller" to show only top sellers
3. Screenshot the top 8 bestselling designs

---

## Step 3: Reverse-Engineer Design Logic

1. Paste the screenshot into Claude
2. Prompt: "Analyze these design images and break down why they are selling"
3. AI identifies emotional triggers (nostalgia, identity, humor) and the underlying success formula
4. **Do NOT copy designs** — understand the *why* behind them

---

## Step 4: Generate Original Design Concepts

1. Claude outputs ~15 original product concepts formatted as image generation prompts
2. Review and select the strongest concepts

---

## Step 5: Create Designs with Nano Banana 2

1. Go to `gemini.google.com`
2. Paste the design prompt
3. Add dimension specification: **3600 x 4800 pixels** for print quality
4. Click "Create Image"
5. Download via the three-dot menu
6. Use "Remove Background" if needed
7. If Printful quality indicator shows "okay" instead of "good," run through an online image upscaler

---

## Step 6: Build Your Independent Store

1. **Do NOT sell on Etsy** — oversaturated with AI designs, algorithm favors established sellers
2. Use **Hostinger** website builder with AI builder feature
3. Describe your store niche — AI generates a customized template
4. Customize images and text (limit to 1-2 hours of decoration time)
5. Set up **Stripe** and **PayPal** as payment methods

---

## Step 7: Connect Printful for Fulfillment

1. In Hostinger, go to store/products section and add Printful integration
2. Set up billing so Printful charges your payment method for production costs on orders
3. Choose a product (e.g., t-shirt), upload Nano Banana design
4. Ensure design fits within the print area
5. Save to templates and add to store
6. Set retail price above Printful base cost (example: retail $15, profit ~$7.50)

---

## Step 8: Market with Organic Content

1. Create short-form video content for TikTok and Instagram
2. Film yourself using the product (wearing the shirt, sipping from a branded mug)
3. Link in bio drives traffic to your store
4. No paid ads needed initially

---

## Key Takeaway

> The entire pipeline from niche idea to live store takes under a day with minimal upfront cost. The key differentiator is reverse-engineering *why* bestsellers work — not copying them — and selling through your own store for better margins and control.
