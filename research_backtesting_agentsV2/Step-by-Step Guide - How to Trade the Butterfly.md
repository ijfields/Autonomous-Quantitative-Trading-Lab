# Step-by-Step Guide: Trading the Iron Butterfly on SPX

**Source:** SMB Capital (YouTube)
**Video ID:** FNKIDMBPcaI
**Upload Date:** 2019-08-19

---

## What This Guide Covers

How to construct and manage an Iron Butterfly on SPX index options — the core income strategy of SMB Capital's trading desk.

---

## Prerequisites

- Options-approved brokerage account with index options access
- Understanding of options basics (puts, calls, strikes, expiration)
- Sufficient margin for defined-risk spreads

---

## Step 1: Identify the ATM Strike

1. Find the current SPX price (e.g., 2815)
2. Select the strike closest to the current price as your center strike
3. This becomes both your short call AND short put strike

---

## Step 2: Select Wing Width

1. Choose equidistant wings above and below (e.g., 25 points)
2. Long call = center + width (2815 + 25 = 2840)
3. Long put = center - width (2815 - 25 = 2790)
4. Wider wings = larger credit but larger max loss

---

## Step 3: Execute the Trade

Simultaneously:
- **Sell** ATM calls (e.g., 10x 2815 calls)
- **Sell** ATM puts (e.g., 10x 2815 puts)
- **Buy** OTM calls (e.g., 10x 2840 calls)
- **Buy** OTM puts (e.g., 10x 2790 puts)

Target: collect credit ≥ 3:1 vs. margin required.

---

## Step 4: Manage the Position

- **Ideal:** SPX stays near center strike through expiration → keep most of credit
- **Professional approach:** Exit or start closing before expiration, not hold to settlement
- **Modify mid-trade** if price moves significantly — adjust wings, roll, or partial close
- **Max profit:** Full credit received (all options expire worthless)
- **Max loss:** Wing width minus credit received (capped and known at entry)

---

## Step 5: Understand Cash Settlement (SPX)

- SPX options settle in cash, not shares
- ITM options pay $100 per point of intrinsic value
- No assignment risk of physical shares

---

## Key Takeaway

> The Iron Butterfly collects a large upfront credit with known, capped max risk. Risk/reward can range from 3:1 to 10:1 depending on wing width and credit received. Professional traders manage positions before expiration rather than holding to settlement. SPX cash settlement eliminates share assignment risk.
