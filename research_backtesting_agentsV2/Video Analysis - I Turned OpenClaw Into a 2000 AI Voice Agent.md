## KEY TOPICS

### The Business Model

| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Setup fee | $1,000 – $2,500 one-time |
| Monthly recurring | $300 – $497/month |
| Platform | GoHighLevel (white-label reselling) |
| AI deployment | Voice AI (inbound calls) + Conversation AI (website chatbot) |
| Client type | Small businesses without AI, websites, or lead automation |

### "AI Guilt" Sales Angle
- Most business owners know they should use AI but don't know how
- Fear competitors are leveraging AI while they're being left behind
- This creates a natural sales opportunity

### GoHighLevel Knowledge Base Setup
1. **Website crawler** — paste URL, tool crawls every page and ingests info automatically
2. **Document uploads** — upload PDFs of SOPs, processes, how-to guides
3. **FAQ section** — add frequently asked questions manually
4. **Master prompt** — governs AI behavior: inbound sales support, lead qualification, appointment booking, customer success

### Two Deployment Modes
1. **Voice AI** — buy a phone number ($1/month), connect to AI agent, handles inbound calls
2. **Conversation AI** — website chatbot widget using same knowledge base

### Client Acquisition: "Foot in the Door" Method
1. Search Google Maps for "[industry] [city]" (e.g., "pool cleaner Dallas")
2. Find businesses with no website (or just a Facebook page)
3. Build them a website using GHL templates (10-15 minutes)
4. Email: "I built this website for a different [industry] company. I'm letting it go for $100/free."
5. After they accept, upsell AI voice agent for $1,000 setup + $297/month
