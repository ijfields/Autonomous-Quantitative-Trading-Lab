### Amygdala Hijack & Trading Psychology (~10% of content)

- **The neural hijack (Daniel Goleman):** Sensory input → thalamus → two paths:
  - Short path: Thalamus → amygdala (instant emotional reaction, no thinking)
  - Long path: Thalamus → prefrontal cortex → amygdala (logical analysis)
- **Trading manifestations:** Revenge trading, panic closing, moving stops, oversizing after wins, choking on triggers
- **Key insight:** "The faster and more volatile the market, the more your amygdala gets triggered — exactly when you need your prefrontal cortex most"
- **Antidote:** Predefined rules, systematic entries/stops/sizing — let logical brain work before the trade, not during
