### Perplexity Computer Architecture
- **Model Orchestrator:** Automatically assigns tasks to the best AI model for each subtask, runs in parallel
- **Connectors:** Integrates with hundreds of apps (similar to Zapier integrations)
- **Scheduled Tasks:** Recurring tasks run in the cloud 24/7
- **Self-QA:** Uses its own browser to verify outputs before delivering them

## KEY TAKEAWAY

> Perplexity Computer positions itself as the "plug-and-play" alternative to OpenClaw — no setup, no security concerns, no local machine required. Its model orchestrator automatically selects the best AI model for each subtask and runs them in parallel. At $20-$200/month, it targets business owners who want AI agents without the complexity of running OpenClaw. The trade-off: less customization and control compared to OpenClaw's open-source flexibility.
