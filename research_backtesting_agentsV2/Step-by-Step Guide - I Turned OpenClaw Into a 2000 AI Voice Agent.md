# Step-by-Step Guide: Building a $2,000/Month AI Voice Agent Business

**Source:** Nick Ponte (YouTube)
**Video ID:** PA8KnvX3S6E
**Upload Date:** 2026-03-11

---

## What This Guide Covers

How to package AI voice agents and chatbots as a service for small businesses using GoHighLevel, with a client acquisition method that starts by building free websites.

---

## Step 1: Set Up GoHighLevel

1. Sign up for GoHighLevel (GHL) — supports white-label reselling
2. Brand the platform under your own company name
3. Familiarize yourself with: CRM, website builder, AI voice + chat agents, phone number provisioning

---

## Step 2: Build the Knowledge Base for AI Agents

1. **Website crawler:** Paste a client's URL — GHL crawls every page automatically
2. **Document uploads:** Upload PDFs of SOPs, processes, and how-to guides
3. **FAQ section:** Manually add frequently asked questions
4. **Master prompt:** Define AI behavior — inbound sales support, lead qualification, appointment booking, customer success

---

## Step 3: Deploy AI Voice Agent

1. Buy a phone number in GHL ($1/month, any area code)
2. Connect it to the AI agent with your knowledge base
3. When someone calls, the voice agent:
   - Introduces itself
   - Answers questions from the knowledge base
   - Qualifies leads
   - Attempts to book appointments
4. Test by calling the number yourself

---

## Step 4: Deploy Website Chatbot

1. In GHL, set up **Conversation AI** with the same knowledge base
2. Add the chatbot widget to the client's website
3. Handles pre-purchase questions and support issues 24/7

---

## Step 5: Find Clients — "Foot in the Door" Method

1. Open Google Maps, search "[industry] [city]" (e.g., "pool cleaner Dallas")
2. Scroll listings for businesses **without a website** (or just a Facebook page)
3. In GHL, choose an industry-specific template
4. Customize it in 10-15 minutes (drag-and-drop) for that specific business
5. Send email with subject: "I built this website for a different [industry] company. I'm letting it go for $100 / free"
6. Include a live link to the already-built site

---

## Step 6: Upsell the AI Agent

1. Once they accept the website, pitch: "Do you have an AI agent booking appointments 24/7?"
2. **Pricing:**
   - Setup fee: $1,000 – $2,500 one-time
   - Monthly maintenance: $300 – $497/month
3. White-label the entire GHL suite under your brand

---

## Step 7: Scale with Recurring Revenue

1. Each client = $300-$500/month recurring revenue
2. 10 clients = $3,000-$5,000/month recurring
3. GoHighLevel handles all infrastructure — you manage relationships and knowledge bases
4. Replicate across industries (plumbers, dentists, lawyers, etc.)

---

## Key Takeaway

> The money isn't in the tool — it's how you deploy it. The same AI agent logic from OpenClaw (structured instructions + knowledge base + clear goals) works as a cloud service that businesses pay $1K+ setup and $300-$500/month for. The "foot in the door" method (building free websites for businesses that don't have one) is the highest-conversion prospecting approach.
