## THE 6 USE CASES

### 1. Second Brain System
- Text anything to OpenClaw from phone (Telegram, iMessage, Discord)
- Bot remembers everything via OpenClaw's memory system
- Built a searchable web UI with Next.js (built by OpenClaw itself)
- Global search (Cmd+K) across all memories, documents, tasks

### 2. Custom Morning Brief
- Scheduled daily at 8:00 AM via Telegram
- Includes: overnight AI news, video ideas with full scripts, Mac to-do list tasks, recommendations for tasks the AI can do itself
- "Saved me several hours a week"

### 3. Content Factory (Discord-based Multi-Agent)
- Agent 1 (Henry): Researches trending stories and content opportunities
- Agent 2 (Quill): Takes research and writes scripts
- Agent 3 (Pixel): Generates AI thumbnails using local Nano Banana on Mac Studio
- Runs at 8:00 AM daily; customizable for tweets, newsletters, etc.

### 4. Market/Product Research
- Uses Matt Van Horde's "Last 30 Days" skill from Claw Hub
- Researches any topic across Reddit and X for the past 30 days
- Identifies pain points, challenges, and opportunities
- Entrepreneurial workflow: find problems → have OpenClaw build a solution → ship it

### 5. Goal-Aligned Autonomous Task Generation
- Brain dump ALL goals, missions, and objectives into OpenClaw
- Every morning at 8:00 AM, it generates 4-5 tasks it can do on your computer toward those goals
- Self-assigns tasks, schedules them, completes them
- "The most important workflow you can do in Open Claw today"

### 6. Build Your Own Mission Control
- Replace SaaS tools (Notion, Todoist, Google Calendar) with custom apps built by OpenClaw
- Custom apps are integrated with OpenClaw's full memory and conversation history
- Saves subscription costs and supercharges tools with AI context

### Cost
- $200/month (Anthropic subscription) for premium
- Cheaper: MiniMax 2.5 (~$10/month), GLM5 (~$5/month)
