## What This Guide Covers

## Step 1: Set Up Three Buckets

| Bucket | Purpose | Target Allocation |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| **Income Accelerators** | High-yield generators | 40-50% |
| **Defensive Edge** | Hedges + diversifiers | 25-30% |
| **Core Investments** | Long-term holds | 25-30% |

## Step 4: Establish Core Holdings

- **QQQI** — Nasdaq income ETF (~$17,500/yr, largest single holding at ~16%)
- **SPYI** — S&P 500 income ETF (~$10,000/yr)
- **DVO** — Dividend value ETF

---

## Step 5: Manage Overlap and Rebalance

1. Avoid tech overlap — Rico exited MAGY because it overlapped with existing tech holdings
2. Trim single-fund exposure when it creates sector concentration
3. Increase defensive positions (gold miners, treasuries) during volatility
4. Volatility is your friend — higher vol = higher option premiums = higher income

---

## Key Takeaway

> A $767K portfolio generating $163K/year ($13,610/month) through options-overlay ETFs across three buckets. The key discipline: manage overlap risk and embrace volatility as an income driver, not a threat.
