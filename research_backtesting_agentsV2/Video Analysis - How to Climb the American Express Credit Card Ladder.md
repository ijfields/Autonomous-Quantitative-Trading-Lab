### The 5 Critical Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **1. Amex Rarely Hard Pulls** | Subsequent applications after first card typically involve no hard inquiry |
| **2. Once-Per-Lifetime** | Each card's welcome bonus given only once, ever (7-10 year "amnesia" possible but don't count on it) |
| **3. Family Language Rule** | Green, Gold, Platinum are one "family" — getting higher card first permanently kills lower-tier bonuses. **Order: Green → Gold → Platinum** |
| **4. Application Speed Limits** | Credit cards: 1 per 5 days, max 2 per 90 days. Pay-over-time cards (Green/Gold/Platinum) are EXEMPT |
| **5. Pop-Up Jail** | If pop-up says "not eligible for welcome offer" — back out immediately, no hard pull occurred. Return after months of heavier spending |

**Bonus Tool:** "Apply with Confidence" pre-approval checker on Amex website (use incognito). If it says approved, you're approved — zero credit impact.
