# Step-by-Step Guide: Systemize Your Business Before Adding AI

**Source:** Ross Harkness (YouTube)
**Video ID:** Ze2Dd_SrGTQ
**Upload Date:** 2026-03-06

---

## What This Guide Covers

How to properly prepare your business for AI integration by systemizing first, then identifying which tasks AI can handle vs. which require human judgment.

---

## Prerequisites

- An existing business with at least a few defined processes
- Willingness to document how things actually work (not how you wish they worked)

---

## Step 1: Accept What AI Can and Can't Do

| AI Can Do Well | AI Cannot Do Well |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Organize existing information | Create original strategy |
| Edit and clean up content | Write in your unique voice |
| Follow defined instructions | Make management decisions |
| Basic data analysis | Brand differentiation |
| SOP building (from your info) | Creative ideation |
| Lead qualification | Product development |

**Rule of thumb:** If you'd give the task to a minimum-wage first-job hire, AI can handle it. If it requires a $50K+ manager, keep it human.

---

## Step 2: Use AI as Amplifier, Not Originator

| Instead of... | Do this... |
|--------------|-----------|
| "AI, create my marketing strategy" | Create strategy yourself → "AI, critique and stress test this" |
| "AI, write my blog post" | Write it yourself → "AI, remove redundancies, improve clarity" |
| "AI, come up with content ideas" | Generate ideas yourself → "AI, organize and expand on these" |
| "AI, build my onboarding process" | Document the process → "AI, turn this into an SOP" |

---

## Step 3: Systemize Your Business

Document these for every major function:

1. **What** the business does (services, products, deliverables)
2. **What processes** exist (onboarding, fulfillment, marketing, sales, support)
3. **How** each process is done (step-by-step)
4. **Who** owns each process
5. **What workflows** connect processes
6. **Performance data** for each process

---

## Step 4: Identify What Can Be Automated

For each documented process, categorize each step:

| Category | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Automatable by software | Use existing tools (Zapier, CRM, etc.) |
| Automatable by AI | Plug in AI for this step |
| Requires human judgment | Keep with a person |

**Key insight:** If a process differs every time you do it, you can't automate it yet. Standardize first.

---

## Step 5: Plug AI Into the Right Places

Current best uses for AI in business:
- Customer support (basic/tier-1)
- Automated reporting
- Lead qualification
- The "last 10%" — editing, analysis, polish
- SOP generation (from your information)

---

## The Bottom Line

> Systemize first. Automate second. AI doesn't fix chaos — it scales it.
