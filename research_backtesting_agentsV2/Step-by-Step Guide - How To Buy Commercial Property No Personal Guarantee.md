## What This Guide Covers

How to use a DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) loan to purchase commercial real estate without personally guaranteeing the loan.

---

## Step 1: Understand DSCR Loans

- **DSCR** = Debt Service Coverage Ratio
- Loan qualification is based on the **property's cash flow**, not your personal credit
- Ideal for buyers with bankruptcy, poor credit, or who want liability protection

---

## Step 2: Evaluate the Property

- Calculate the property's **Net Operating Income (NOI)**
- Ensure NOI covers: new loan payments + all fixed expenses
- If the property's cash flow services the debt → you can qualify

---

## Step 3: Prepare Your Down Payment

- Required equity: **20-25%** of purchase price
- This is non-negotiable — you need skin in the game even without a personal guarantee

---

## Key Takeaway

> As long as the property's income covers the debt and expenses, you can get a loan based strictly on the cash flow — no personal guarantee needed.
